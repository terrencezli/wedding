• REBECCA & TERRENCE

    July 28, 2018

OUR STORY

Rebecca and Terrence met in high school. Terrence brought Rebecca hot chocolate every morning for two straight months, and most days she gave it to her friend. At the end of the school year, Rebecca asked for Terrence's number (just as friends, she claims).

After years of trips up and down the state between the Bay Area, SLO, and Irvine, Terrence decided to propose the next time Rebecca would be home. Arriving three hours later than expected, Rebecca said yes while holding a bag of In-N-Out and family and friends waiting inside to celebrate.
